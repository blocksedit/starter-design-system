Writing

#Mechanics

  • ℹ️ Subject Line - the first impression of what the message is about, enticing the reader to open the message
  • ℹ️ Preheader - short summary text following the subject line when viewing an email in an inbox
  • ❌ Avoid all caps and punctuation in subject lines, except for dashes and colons
  • ✅ Keep the preheader text short and simple; use to tell readers what the email is about, why it’s relevant, and to support your subject line

#Content

  • ✅ Keep the length of the email short, concise, about one page maximum in length. Newsletters may be longer, but use links to share further details.
  • ✅ Use bullet points to identify key points in the message and repeat the main message throughout the campaign
  • ✅ A Call to Action is important for persuading readers to click on your content
  • ✅ Be clear and concise in your CTA
  • ✅ Build contextual content around your CTA

#UTM parameters

All links should be tagged using the appropriate UTM parameters, including linked images. Here's how:

https://blocksedit.com/starter-email-design-system/?utm_source=email-newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=04-2020&utm_content=logo
  • Website URL
  • utm_source - referrer
  • utm_medium - marketing campaign type, always "email" in this case
  • utm_campaign - name for current email campaign
  • utm_content - content piece it came from, optional
